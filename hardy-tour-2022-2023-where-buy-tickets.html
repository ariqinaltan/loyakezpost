Hardy Tour 2022-2023: Where can I buy tickets?

August 2024 · 3 minute read

HARDY announced he will be going on tour in 2023 following the release of his newest album, the mockingbird & THE CROW.

His next tour will take place shortly after the conclusion of his 2022 tour which will end in December.

When is Hardy going on tour?

Michael Wilson Hardy, who professionally goes by Hardy, is a versatile country music singer and songwriter.

He announced he will be going on his 2023 tour which will kick off in Indiana in February.

The tour will pick up two months after he concludes his current tour which began on August 25, 2022.

However, Hardy canceled his upcoming tour dates through December 1, following a bus crash on October 2.

He told fans that three of the four people on the bus, including himself, were injured but are recovering while their driver, Ricky, is "not in the clear yet."

The canceled dates are October 8 in Arlington, Texas, and October 13 in Columbus, Ohio but he confirmed the October 13 show will be moved to July 1, 2023.

His tour will pick back up on December 1 and the tour will conclude in South Carolina on December 16, 2022.

Hardy's 2023 tour will kick off in February 2023, following the release of his newest album, which will feature a mix of rock and country music.

His album, the mockingbird & THE CROW, will be released on January 20, 2023.

“I’m incredibly grateful to be able to bring this record to you next year. Touring it is something I’ve been thinking about since we started the recording process,” Hardy told Substream Magazine.

“This album is my best work so far. I truly believe that, and I want this tour to reflect that every night. Excited to have my buddies Blame My Youth and Jameson out with me; it’s going to be crazy.”
