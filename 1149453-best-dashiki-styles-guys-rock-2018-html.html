Dashiki, perhaps, is one of the brightest and most recognizable elements of traditional African styles. Nigerian men use dashiki designs with pleasure in their daily and festive looks as well as other native styles.

Dashiki designs

Most often, we can find danshiki styles of straight men's tunics. They have a length up to the middle of the thigh with long, three-quarter or short sleeves and small cuts on the sides. Also, this style is widely used for T-shirts making.

There are dashiki options also in the form of a classic shirt with cuffs and a breast pocket.
