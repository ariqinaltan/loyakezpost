Iggy Azalea and Nick Young postpone wedding

August 2024 · 3 minute read

    Iggy Azalea has been busy planning her wedding to fiancé Nick Young since the pair got engaged last summer. Just recently, the singer revealed she had begun the process of creating her dream dress with none other than designer Giorgio Armani.

    But it seems fans will have to wait a little longer to see the blushing bride tie the knot as the couple have postponed their wedding until next year.

    Iggy Azalea has revealed she has postponed her wedding to Nick Young

    "I'm planning a wedding. I actually moved my wedding because I have to tour, so my wedding plans are changing," she said during an interview on Friday.

    "It was gonna happen at the end of the year, but now I have to go to work. We'll probably have to wait until the end of the next basketball season to do that. So that kind of is a bit sucky, but it's all right."

    The Australian rapper and her Lakers player fiancé have also had to push back their honeymoon which had already been booked.

    The rapper and the Lakers player became engaged in 2015

    "We'd already booked our honeymoon and everything, so it was kind of good, 'cause at least now we moved our honeymoon up, so now I get a free vacation kind of thing," she told Ryan laughing. "Now our honeymoon is just gonna be our vacation, and then I'll pick another honeymoon."

    The 25-year-old, who just released her new single appeared on the show to talk about the tough time she has been through in recent months but confessed she is thankful that her partner understands that they have to push back the wedding so the singer can go on tour.

    "Luckily, I have Nick, who's really awesome and great and totally understanding and does not mind: He's like, 'I can be a bachelor for longer!' He's really milking the whole thing."

    Nick Young proposed to Iggy in June 2015. The Los Angeles Lakers star popped the question at his 30th birthday party, getting down on bended knee in front of his friends and family.

    The couple will tie the knot next year

    The singer later shared the happy news with her fans, sharing a picture which showed off her stunning ten-carat diamond ring.

    "Love you @swaggyp1," Iggy captioned the snap. A second selfie showed the lovebirds beaming at the camera, with Nick looking particularly thrilled that his sweetheart had said "yes".
