Andrew Dice Clay is one of the prolific celebrities in the United States who makes a reasonable sum through his acting and comedy in the entertainment industry.

Clay began his career in Hollywood in 1978 as a comedian. However, his career breakthrough came in 1988, when he performed in the presence of Hollywood celebrities at a dinner hosted entirely by the Big Brother Association (BBA). He was then thrust into the national spotlight after his seven-minute performance in New York in 1988. Clay also performed at the 1989 MTV Video Music Awards.

As an actor, he appeared in over 50 films. His first film role was in Wacko, in 1981.
