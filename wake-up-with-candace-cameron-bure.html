According to Bure's longtime stylist, Erin Noël, 'Candace's style is effortless, casual, and feminine. She epitomizes everything California but always showcases trends and styles that appeal to her personality – fun, colorful, and [of course] fashion-forward.' 

Case in point – her Wake up with me... attire. 

The 44-year-old remained reminiscent of her signature 'California-cool' fashion in our video – and throughout her morning of press interviews – providing a first-hand tutorial on how to master the art of WFH-style effortlessly.  

To emphasize her blonde hair and blue eyes, she opted for a marvellous magenta blend for her top. The pop of color packed a powerful punch and balanced the rest of her ensemble out to perfection, complementing gorgeous gold-toned jewels.
