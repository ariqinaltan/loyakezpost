Burna Boys grandfather and Fela's former manager Benson Idonije clocks 84 (photos)

- The veteran broadcaster and Afrobeat pioneer Fela’s former manager, clocked 84 on June 13, 2020

- To mark the occasion, Burna Boy’s mother and manager, Bose Ogulu, took to social media to pen down a sweet note to celebrate him

Birthdays are very special events that come only once a year. Despite that, it is usually looked forward to as the celebrants know that they would be given an even more special treatment on that day, especially if they lived a good life.

Nigerian top singer, Burna Boy’s grandfather, Benson Idonije, clocked a year older to the joy of fans and well-wishers.

The veteran broadcaster, journalist and music ciritc marked his 84th birthday on June 13, 2020.

To make the event a memorable one, the celebrant’s daughter, Bose Ogulu, who also happens to be Burna Boy’s mother and manager, took to her Instagram page to pen down sweet words for her father.

The proud daughter shared a throwback photo of her father alongside more recent ones taken on his birthday. Present at the celebration was Burna Boy, his father Samuel Ogulu, his sister, and other well-wishers.

Bose described her father as a Pan Africanist, a veteran broadcaster, music critic, journalist and veteran deejay.

In her words: “PanAfricanist, veteran broadcaster, veteran deejay, journalist, award winning music critic, founding manager of Koola Lobitos,

My father..... Happy 84th birthday to you.

Vive L’encyclopedie musicale. Many more years.”
