The Story of Inspector Avinash Season 1

Inspector Avinash is set in the year 1990 and follows the story of Avinash, a fearless police officer portrayed by Randeep Hooda. He finds himself in a relentless battle against the arms mafia, turning into a determined “encounter specialist.”
