You Wanna Get Between Texas' Two Tail-Wagging Cities? We Got You Covered (Mileage-Wise, Not Literally)

Ah, Texas. Land of wide-open spaces, big steaks, and...well, even bigger questions. Like, for instance, how far apart exactly are San Antonio and Austin? Those two Texas titans might be twins when it comes to keepin' things weird, but how close are they geographically?

Hold Your Horses (Literally and Figuratively)

Before we lasso the answer, let's address the elephant in the room (or maybe it's a steer? Texas, after all). The distance between these two Texas gems depends entirely on how you plan to hightail it from one to the other.

As the Armadillo Flies

If you're a particularly determined armadillo (or maybe a champion pigeon), the straight-line distance, also known as the "as the crow flies" distance, is approximately 73.59 miles. But hey, let's be real, most of us aren't packing wings or digging tunnels (unless you're a particularly ambitious roadrunner).

Hitting the Open Road (or I-35, to be Specific)

For us regular folks on four wheels, the distance between San Antonio and Austin is a manageable 79-80 miles via the ever-reliable Interstate 35. Buckle up, crank up the tunes, and be prepared for a trip that shouldn't take much longer than 1 hour and 20 minutes (depending on how heavy your foot is and how many kolaches you snag along the way).

The Scenic Route (Because Why Not?)

Sure, I-35 might be the fastest route, but if you're feeling fancy (or just want to avoid traffic), there are always other options. Just be prepared to add some extra miles to your odometer. But hey, who knows? You might just discover a hidden gem of a barbeque joint or a quirky roadside attraction along the way.

Okay, Okay, Enough Already, How Far Is It?

Alright, alright, settle down. Here's the short and sweet answer:

  • By car (via I-35): 79-80 miles
  • As the crow flies: 73.59 miles (but seriously, who flies?)

Bonus: How Long Does it Take?

By car, the drive should take about 1 hour and 20 minutes, depending on traffic and your pit stop strategy.

Frequently Asked Texan Traveler Questions:

How to Get from San Antonio to Austin?

Car is the most common way, but there are also buses and trains available.

Around 1 hour and 20 minutes via I-35.

What's the Most Scenic Route Between San Antonio and Austin?

There are several options, but be prepared for a longer trip.

What Snacks Should I Pack for the Drive?

Texas-sized snacks, of course! Kolaches, jerky, and maybe some Big Red for good measure.

What Radio Station Should I Listen to on the Drive?

Anything that plays Texas Country, duh.
