Miles Morales is back for the latest chapter of the animated Spider-Man series, and you can now watch Spider-Man: Across the Spider-Verse from home.

After hitting theaters in June, Across the Spider-Verse arrived on Blu-ray and DVD on Sept. 5 and quickly hit number one on Amazon’s bestselling movies chart.

Amazon’s current deal has the Spider-Man DVD on sale for under $20. You can also get a 4K HDR version of the film on Blu-ray for $29.96 (as of this writing). Every physical purchase includes a digital download of the film that’s yours to keep, so you can stream Across the Spider-Verse on your TV or laptop on-demand.

Starring Shameik Moore, Hailee Steinfeld, Brian Tyree Henry, Jake Johnson and Daniel Kaluuya, among others, Spider-Man: Across the Spider-Verse was a massive hit at the box office, debuting at $120.7 million. That made it the second-highest opening weekend total of 2023 so far, behind The Super Mario Bros. Movie. 

The Blu-ray version of Across the Spider-Verse features 90 minutes of special features, including deleted scenes, obscure easter eggs and commentary from the cast and crew. The physical release also includes a behind-the-scenes look at the animation process for the film, previously unheard tracks from the soundtrack, and a featurette called “Escape from Spider Society.”

While the film is now available on Blu-ray/DVD, Sony has yet to release a streaming date for Across the Spider-Verse. 2021’s Spider-Man: No Way Home is currently streaming on Hulu (as is 2018’s Spider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse), but it’s not clear when the latest film will hit streaming services.

For now, you can stream Spider-Man: Across the Spider-Verse online by renting it on Prime Video.
