How To Watch Vmas On Hulu

August 2024 · 2 minute read
People are currently reading this guide.

Yo! VMAs on Hulu: Your Guide to Not Missing Out (Unless You, Like, Totally Forget)

So, the MTV VMAs are coming up, and you're itching to see who snags the coveted Moon Person trophies (because let's be honest, those things are way cooler than an Oscar). But here's the thing: cable? Feels so 2002. Fear not, my friend, because we're here to spill the tea on how to watch the VMAs on the glorious platform that is Hulu.

But First, a Public Service Announcement (PSA):

Double-check the date, people! We all know 2023 flew by in a blur of questionable fashion choices and celebrity beefs, but the VMAs are typically held in late summer, around August or September. This post is evergreen, though, so file it away in your mental rolodex for future reference.

Hulu to the Rescue: Your Ticket to Award Show Glory (and Maybe Some Awkward Acceptance Speeches)

Alright, now that we've established you're not about to accidentally watch reruns of The Hills (although, let's be real, that's not always a bad thing), here's the good stuff:

  • Hulu with Live TV: Your One-Stop Shop for VMAs and Beyond

This bad boy is your key to unlocking the VMAs live stream. Here's the deal: Hulu with Live TV includes MTV, so you can catch the red carpet, the epic performances, and all the acceptance speeches that will inevitably go viral (for better or worse). Plus, you get a ton of other channels to keep you entertained – you know, in case Beyonce takes a bathroom break and you need a commercial break distraction.

Heads up: There is a monthly fee for Hulu with Live TV, but some sweet deals might be floating around, so do your research, friend.

Here's the Super-Technical Stuff (but We Promise It's Painless)

Downloading the Hulu app is pretty much a no-brainer. Once you're logged in and have your Hulu with Live TV subscription rocking, just find MTV on the channel guide when the VMAs air. Easy peasy, lemon squeezy.

Bonus Tip: Set a reminder on your phone so you don't miss a single booty shake or epic guitar solo.

So Basically, You're All Set to Be a VMAs Viewing Pro

There you have it! With Hulu, you're all prepped to witness the magic (and maybe some minor meltdowns) of the VMAs. Now go forth, grab some snacks (extra points for popcorn!), and get ready for a night of outrageous outfits, killer performances, and enough pop culture moments to fuel your social media posts for weeks.
