I Restocked My Underwear Drawer With These Comfy Amazon Panties

August 2024 · 3 minute read

Everyone knows my favorite pastime is shopping, but there’s one category of my wardrobe that’s in dire need of a makeover: my underwear. While my efforts have been focused on curating the perfect winter wardrobe, the elastic bands on my panties have lost all of their stretch. They fit way too tight, and just aren’t in great shape overall, so I ended up tossing over half my collection. 

I’ve been pushing off purchasing new underwear for months, but after my most recent laundry load, I realized I was down to a handful of wearable styles. So, I finally stopped procrastinating and took my shopping expertise to Amazon to peruse its selection of multi-pack undies. I came across Yadifen’s Five-Pack of Cotton Bikini Underwear, and my first impressions? They fit and feel better than some of my pricier panties, given that they’re only $2 apiece.

The bikini-cut underwear comes in a five- or 10-pack of all black, pastels, or darker shades. Sizes S through XXL are all equally stretchy thanks to a cotton-spandex blend. The underwear has a breathable double-crotch lining and elastic at both the waist and leg openings for a bulk-free comfortable and secure fit that moves with you.

Yadifen Five-Pack Cotton Bikini Underwear

The bikini underwear offers great coverage, so I’m not walking around with a wedgie all day, and the fabric wears seamlessly under jeans and dresses without any added bulkiness. It’s also lightweight and breathable, ensuring I feel fresh, dry, and comfortable all day, and is unbelievably soft to avoid irritating the skin. I’ve machine-washed mine multiple times, and they’re incredibly durable — even without washing in an intimates bag.  

Another noteworthy factor is the fabric's stretchiness, which contributes to an accommodating fit. The leg holes are wide enough to offer freedom of movement without restriction, and the elastic waistband stays in place, so you don’t have to deal with annoying rolling. 

And I’m not the only fan of Yadifen’s bikini underwear. It has nearly 2,000 five-star ratings with raving reviews to match. One shopper said these were great “no-frill undergarments” and liked that they didn’t “require any special care instructions.” They also said the trim is “well-stitched” and “doesn’t irritate [their] very sensitive skin” like other brands. Another reviewer said these panties are “very stretchy,” fit “snugly but not too tightly,” and don’t “create lines under her leggings. A final shopper who works as a sports coach said the underwear is “durable,” provides good coverage, and “doesn’t ride up.”
