So, You Want to Count Urbana's Residents? We've Got You Covered (But Maybe Not Literally)

Ah, Urbana, Illinois. Twin city to Champaign, home to the prestigious University of Illinois (where, let's be honest, most of the interesting people are), and a place with a population that's just... well, it depends on who you ask.

The Official Headcount: Not Too Shabby, But Not Quite a Metropolis Either

The 2020 census, the most recent official tally, clocked Urbana's population in at around 38,336. That's a pretty decent-sized town, enough folks to fill a stadium (or a few libraries, which Urbana seems to have in abundance).

But here's the thing: Urbana and Champaign are practically joined at the hip. They're like peanut butter and jelly, Batman and Robin, Beyoncé and Coachella (okay, that last one might be a stretch). So, some folks just consider them one big, happy metro area.

The Metropolitan Menagerie: We're Counting Chickens (and Students) Here!

If you peek at the Champaign-Urbana metropolitan area, things get a bit more crowded. As of 2022, estimates suggest there are around 236,514 people living the Champaign-Urbana life. That's a significant jump! Though, to be fair, a good chunk of that population surge can probably be attributed to the University of Illinois's ever-expanding student body. Those college kids, always multiplying like... well, college students.

So, what's the big takeaway? Urbana itself has a population that's on the smaller side, but the combined Champaign-Urbana area is a bustling little metropolis.

Urbana Population: Fun Facts (Because We All Need a Laugh)

  • Urbana's population density is higher than a frat house on a Saturday night.
  • If everyone in Urbana held hands, they'd create a human chain that would stretch for... well, let's just say it would be a pretty long chain.
  • You're more likely to run into a Nobel laureate in Urbana than a herd of cattle (though there is that famous cowbell tradition at the football games...).

How Many People Live in Urbana, Illinois? FAQ

1. How to count the population of Urbana?

Unless you're up for a city-wide headcount yourself (not recommended, trust us), it's best to rely on official sources like the US Census Bureau.

2. How big is the Urbana metropolitan area?

The Champaign-Urbana metropolitan area boasts a population of around 236,514, according to 2022 estimates.

3. Is Urbana a good place to live?

With a vibrant college town atmosphere, a strong job market, and plenty of cultural attractions, Urbana offers a pretty sweet quality of life.

4. What's the difference between Urbana and Champaign?

They're practically twins! They share the University of Illinois and have a lot in common, but Urbana is the county seat.

5. Will Urbana's population grow in the future?

With the University of Illinois as a major draw, Urbana's population is likely to see continued growth. Just don't expect it to become the next Chicago anytime soon.
