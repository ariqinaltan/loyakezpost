Emily Compagno’s Career, Professions & Lifestyle

She soon started her professional life after receiving her J.D. degree. For many years, Emily worked as a criminal defense lawyer in San Francisco. Alongwith this, Compagno also performed as a captain of the Raiderettes (Oakland Raiders cheerleading team).

Her next job was at the Social Security Administration where she worked as a federal attorney. In 2018, Compagno joined Fox News and began working as a contributor and legal analyst. 

On March 9, 2020, her show Crimes That Changed America was debuted. On January 25, 2021, Emily Compagno became a permanent co-host of Outnumbered.
