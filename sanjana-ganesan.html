Who is Sanjana Ganesan? Husband, Parents, Wiki, Biography, Age, Height, Net Worth

August 2024 · 7 minute read

Sanjana Ganesan (born 6 May 1991, Age: 32 years old) is a popular Indian model and sports anchor who works for Star Sports, one of the leading sports channels in the country. She is also the wife of Jasprit Bumrah, the Indian fast bowler who is considered one of the best in the world.

The couple tied the knot in a private ceremony in Goa on March 15, 2021, and welcomed their first child, a son named Angad Jasprit Bumrah, on September 4, 2023. In this article, we will take a closer look at Ganesan’s life, career, achievements, and net worth.

Education & Early Life

Sanjana was born on Monday, 6 May 1991, in Pune, Maharashtra. Her zodiac sign is Taurus. She belongs to the Kallar caste, a Tamil warrior community. She grew up in a well-educated and supportive family. Her father, Ganesan Ramaswamy, is a management guru and an author. Her mother, Dr. Sushma Ganesan, is an advocate and a fitness coach. Her younger sister, Sheetal Ganesan, is a dentist.

Sanjana completed her schooling at The Bishop’s School, Pune, where she was a bright student. She then pursued B.Tech in Software Engineering from the Symbiosis Institute of Technology, Pune, in 2012, and graduated as a gold medalist. She was also interested in modeling and participated in several beauty pageants during her college days.

Sanjana had a keen interest in modeling and fashion since her college days. She participated in several beauty pageants and fashion shows, such as the 2012 Femina Style Diva, the 2013 Femina Miss India Pune, and the 2013 Femina Officially Gorgeous, where she won or became a finalist. She also appeared in the reality TV show MTV Splitsvilla Season 7 in 2014, where she had a brief relationship with co-contestant Ashwini Koul. However, she had to quit the show due to an injury and later broke up with Ashwini.

Professional Career

Ganesan has had a diverse and successful career as a model, software engineer, and sports anchor. She has worked for various platforms and events, showcasing her talent, knowledge, and personality. Here are some of the highlights of her career journey.

Modeling

Sanjana started her modeling career while she was still in college. She participated in several beauty pageants and fashion shows and won many accolades and recognition. Some of her achievements in the modeling field are:

  • She was a finalist in the Femina Style Diva 2012 competition, where she impressed the judges with her style and confidence.
  • She was a finalist in the Femina Miss India Pune 2013 competition, where she competed with other aspiring models from the city.
  • She won the Femina Officially Gorgeous 2013 contest, where she proved her beauty and grace.

Sanjana Ganesan also did various photo shoots and advertisements for various brands and products. She was featured in magazines like Femina, Grazia, and L’Officiel.

Software Engineering

Sanjana completed her B.Tech in Software Engineering from the Symbiosis Institute of Technology, Pune, in 2012. She graduated as a gold medalist, proving her academic excellence. She then joined CDK Global, an IT and digital marketing company, as a software engineer in 2013. She worked there for a year, developing and testing software solutions for various clients. However, she soon realized that her passion was in the entertainment industry and quit her job to pursue her dream.

Sports Anchoring

Sanjana Ganesan got a breakthrough in her career when she was hired by Star Sports, one of the leading sports channels in India, as an anchor and sports presenter in 2016. She said in an interview that Star Sports was a happy accident and it happened out of nowhere. She started hosting various sports events, such as cricket, badminton, football, and poker. She gained recognition and fame for her hosting skills, knowledge, and personality.

Some of the notable events and shows that she has hosted are:

  • The 2019 Cricket World Cup from the Indian side, where she interviewed and interacted with various players, experts, and fans.
  • The Indian Premier League, the most popular and lucrative cricket league in the world, covered the matches, analysis, and updates.
  • The Badminton Premier League, the premier badminton league in India, showcased the action and excitement of the sport.
  • The Indian Super League, the top-tier football league in India, brought passion and enthusiasm to the game.
  • The Pro Kabaddi League, the professional kabaddi league in India, highlighted the thrill and skill of the sport.
  • The Poker Sports League was the first poker league in India, where she introduced the strategy and fun of the game.

She also worked as a digital insider for the International Cricket Council and hosted the 2020 Women’s T20 World Cup and the 2022 Men’s T20 World Cup. She also hosted the ICC Awards of the Decade in 2020, where she announced the winners of various categories.

Marriage with Jasprit Bumrah

Ganesan kept her personal life private and did not reveal much about her relationship with Jasprit Bumrah. The couple reportedly dated for two years before getting married on March 15, 2021, in a private ceremony in Goa. They followed the traditional Sikh rituals and invited only their close family and friends. Bumrah’s colleagues from the Indian cricket team could not attend the wedding as they were in a bio-bubble for the ongoing T20I series against England in Ahmedabad.

The couple announced their marriage on social media and shared photos from their wedding ceremony. They wrote, “Steered by love, we have begun a new journey together. Today is one of the happiest days of our lives and we feel blessed to be able to share the news of our wedding and our joy with you. Jasprit & Sanjana.”

On September 4, 2023, the couple welcomed their first child, a son named Angad Jasprit Bumrah. They shared the news on social media and wrote, “We are over the moon to share that we have been blessed with a beautiful baby boy. We thank you all for your love, prayers, and good wishes. Angad and Sanjana.”
